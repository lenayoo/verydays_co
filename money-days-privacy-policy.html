Money Days Privacy Policy

Money Days is a simple budget app designed to keep your records on your device and out of tracking systems.

No sign-in On-device records No analytics SDK

Summary

Money Days is a simple budget app for recording spending and income without accounts or behavioral tracking. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or payment credentials.

Information We Collect

Money Days does not collect personal information or device-based identifiers that can be used to identify you. The spending and income entries you create are intended to remain stored locally on your device.

How We Use Information

The information you enter is used only inside the app on your device to show records, category totals, and monthly summaries. We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Third-Party Services and Sharing

Money Days does not include advertising SDKs, external analytics tools, or third-party tracking technologies. If you choose to use a system share or export feature, only the content you choose to share is handled by the app or service you selected, under that service's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Because Money Days does not collect personal information from users, it also does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the developer through the contact method provided on the App Store listing.
